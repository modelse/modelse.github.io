About Us!

Angie grew up in Michigan with her four sisters, Toni, Nadga, Mary and Wendy on a farm outside of Bronson. She graduated from the University of Michigan in 1979 with a Bachelor of Science in Zoology. Her four children were born in Texas and they moved to Washington in 1998. Andrew, the oldest, is living in San Francisco with his wife Rebecca and their dog Ruby. Austin has remained in Washington, with his wife Leanne and three beautiful little girls, Ella, Lily, and Juliet Rose. The twins, Eve and Emily, reside in Washington and Oregon respectively. Angie is working at the Fred Hutchinson Cancer Research Center in cancer prevention research and has been working there for the last 15 years.

Mark hails from the San Francisco Bay area. He grew up with one sister, Lori, in San Bruno just outside The City. After graduating from the University of Southern California with a degree in Mechanical Engineering, he was commissioned as an Ensign in the United States Navy in 1979 with orders to the an A-6 squadron, VA- 115, aboard the USS Midway homeported in Yokosuka, Japan, where he was a Maintenance Officer for 3+ years. Upon receiving orders to flight training in Pensacola Florida, Mark went on to fly the EA-6B Prowler aboard the USS Forrestal. Following 10 years active duty, he went in to the Naval Reserve, retiring from the Navy in 1999. He began work at McDonnell Aircraft Company in 1989, working for three years, moving on to Boeing and moving to the Pacific Northwest, now with the Company for over 28 years.

Mark and Angie met in 2011 as they were getting out of their cars on the way to a cooking class! Mark remembers seeing Angie, thinking “this is going to be fun”. Well, they started talking right off and knew there was something special. This was followed by dancing, dining, cooking and travel as their Love grew. They have travelled together to the East Coast, Mexico and Hawaii as well as a very special trip to Italy. So now, they are building a home together in Maple Valley and are looking forward to having a long future together spending more time with family and friends, traveling, dancing and …. to be continued.
